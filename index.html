The Purpose Of Recycling

The main reason of recycling is to conserve energy, help the environment, reduce pollution, slow global warming and lower waste products in landfills. By recycling, people can have something to do with the earth's overall health and keep the air, water and land clean.

Recycle

Recycling helps protect the environment. Recycling reduces the need for extracting (mining, quarrying and logging), refining and processing raw materials all of which create substantial air and water pollution. Because recycling saves energy, it also reduces greenhouse gas emissions, helping to tackle climate change.
